McGill CSUS

Computer Science Undergraduate Society

Welcome to the CSUS website! The CSUS is an elected student group tasked with improving student academics and life in the computer science department. This includes discussing course changes with faculty, organising events, collating student feedback, and promoting a sense of community. Meet your 2016-2017 CSUS members:
